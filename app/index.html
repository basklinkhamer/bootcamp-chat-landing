Desktop application

The application runs straight out of the box on OSX, Windows and Linux. Due to the magic of electron we can bind a regular web-project to a native window on your desktop! When you're finished, you can create your own installer.

Go ahead and customize

This app was built to provide a platform to play with and build on, no matter the amount of experience. Change colours and fonts, or add emoji integration or slash commands. We've started you off with Gravatars and native notifications.

Where to next?

Our session aims to show everyone how versatily web-tech can be outside of the browser. You can adjust the app to suit just you, or launch a pull-request and share with everyone. What to think about a tablet version for instance?
